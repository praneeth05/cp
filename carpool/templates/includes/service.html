REMOVING HARD WATER STAINS

Removing hard water spots from your vehicle body and glass surface.

This is because when the water from a sprinkler system or rain in the city falls on your vehicle, the water contains contaminants.

As the water on the surface evaporates away, the contaminants adhere themselves to the paint or windows. Once this occurs, they become extremely difficult to remove. Our Acid Rain and Hard Water Stain Removal service is designed to solve this problem.

EXTERIOR CAR DETAILING

(Rub, polish and waxing)

Car Washing: The exterior car detailing process begins with an intensive washing using car shampoo.

Surface Claying:After the washing, the paintwork is then clayed using the clay bar. It removes any of the dirt that didn’t get off with wash.

Rubbing & Polishing: After cleaning the car in every manner the process of car polishing is performed. The original polish on the paint surface is removed and a new protective wax coat is applied either manually or using machines.

Paint Sealing: Paint sealing is done to protect the car using purpose-made car sealant or car wax. This is done to restore the brand new showroom shine of the car that it might’ve lost over the years.

Headlights & Taillights and misc:The headlights, as well as the taillights, are cleaned for better illumination.
